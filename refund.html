1. Overview

At WaveTravel, we strive to provide reliable intercity cab services and ensure customer satisfaction. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds are processed for our services. We aim to be fair and transparent in all refund-related matters.

Important: All refunds are processed through the original payment method used for booking. Refunds may take 5-7 business days to reflect in your account, depending on your bank or payment provider.

3. Full Refund Scenarios

You are eligible for a 100% refund (excluding payment gateway charges) in the following situations:

  • Service Failure: When we fail to provide the booked cab service due to reasons attributable to WaveTravel
  • Driver No-Show: If the assigned driver fails to arrive at the pickup location within 30 minutes of scheduled time without valid reason
  • Vehicle Breakdown: If the assigned vehicle breaks down and we cannot provide an alternative within 1 hour
  • Safety Concerns: If safety standards are not met or the vehicle/driver does not match our quality standards
  • Overcharging: If you are charged more than the agreed fare due to system error
  • Service Cancellation by WaveTravel: If we cancel the service due to operational reasons

4. Partial Refund Scenarios

Partial refunds may be applicable in the following cases:

  • Route Change: If the actual distance is significantly less than initially calculated
  • Early Trip Termination: If the trip is terminated early due to circumstances beyond our control
  • Service Issues: For service quality issues that don't warrant a full refund but require compensation
  • Promotional Discrepancies: If promotional discounts were not applied correctly

5. No Refund Policy

No refunds will be provided in the following situations:
  • Cancellation less than 2 hours before pickup time
  • Customer no-show or failure to contact driver/WaveTravel
  • Changes in personal plans or schedule
  • Weather conditions (unless service is suspended by authorities)
  • Traffic delays beyond reasonable estimates
  • Customer-requested route changes that increase the trip cost
  • Refusal to travel due to personal reasons after driver arrival
  • Completed trips (payment covers the service rendered)

7. Payment Gateway Charges

Payment gateway charges (typically 1.5-3% of transaction amount) levied by payment processors like Razorpay are non-refundable except in cases where the fault is entirely attributable to WaveTravel. These charges are deducted from the refund amount where applicable.

8. Refund Methods

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Refunds are credited back to the original card within 5-7 business days
  • Net Banking: Refunds are processed to the originating bank account within 5-7 business days
  • Digital Wallets: Refunds are credited to the original wallet within 3-5 business days
  • UPI: Refunds are processed instantly to 24 hours depending on the bank
  • WaveTravel Wallet: Instant credit to your WaveTravel wallet for future bookings

10. Force Majeure

WaveTravel shall not be liable for refunds in cases of force majeure including but not limited to:

  • Natural disasters, floods, earthquakes
  • Government-imposed lockdowns or travel restrictions
  • Strikes, bandhs, or civil unrest
  • Acts of terrorism or war
  • Pandemic-related restrictions

In such cases, we may provide credits for future use or reschedule services without additional charges.
