Pricing Policy

WaveTravel - Transparent & Fair Pricing

1. Pricing Overview

WaveTravel is committed to providing transparent, fair, and competitive pricing for our intercity cab services. Our pricing is designed to offer excellent value while ensuring the highest quality of service and safety standards.

Our Promise: No hidden charges, transparent pricing, and upfront cost calculation before booking confirmation.

2. Pricing Structure

2.1 Base Pricing Components

Our pricing includes the following components:

  • Distance Charge: Calculated based on actual kilometers traveled
  • Time Charge: For waiting time and traffic delays
  • Vehicle Type: Different rates for sedan, SUV, and premium vehicles
  • Service Fee: Platform and booking processing fee
  • Taxes: Applicable GST and state taxes

2.2 Vehicle Categories & Rates

Vehicle Category Base Rate (per km) Waiting Charges (per hour) Night Charges (10 PM - 6 AM)
Economy Sedan (4 seater) ₹12-15 per km ₹150 per hour 25% additional
Premium Sedan (4 seater) ₹15-18 per km ₹180 per hour 25% additional
SUV (6-7 seater) ₹18-22 per km ₹200 per hour 25% additional
Luxury Vehicle ₹25-30 per km ₹300 per hour 25% additional
Note: Actual rates may vary based on distance, route, demand, and seasonal factors. Final pricing will be displayed before booking confirmation.

3. Additional Charges

3.1 Waiting Time

  • First 15 minutes of waiting at pickup location: Free
  • Beyond 15 minutes: Charged as per vehicle category rates
  • En-route stops: ₹100 per stop (maximum 15 minutes per stop)

3.2 Route and Distance

  • Distance calculated via GPS and shortest route
  • Toll charges: Actual toll fees (if applicable)
  • Parking charges: Actual parking fees (if applicable)
  • State border charges: As applicable

3.3 Special Circumstances

Special Charge Type Rate Description
Airport Pickup/Drop ₹100-200 Additional convenience fee for airport services
Outstation Return Return fare applicable For one-way bookings, return empty run charges
Driver Accommodation ₹500 per night For multi-day trips requiring driver stay
Cleaning Fee ₹500-2000 If vehicle requires special cleaning after trip

4. Surge Pricing

4.1 When Surge Pricing Applies

Surge pricing may be implemented during:

  • Peak travel seasons (holidays, festivals)
  • High demand periods
  • Limited vehicle availability
  • Extreme weather conditions
  • Special events in destination cities

4.2 Surge Pricing Notification

Customers will be notified of surge pricing before booking confirmation. Surge multipliers typically range from 1.2x to 2.0x of base rates. You can choose to proceed or wait for normal pricing.

5. Payment Terms

5.1 Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Rupay, Amex)
  • Net Banking (all major banks)
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
  • Digital Wallets (Paytm, Amazon Pay, etc.)
  • Cash (subject to availability and route)

5.2 Payment Schedule

  • Advance Payment: 25-50% advance required for booking confirmation
  • Balance Payment: Can be paid before or during the trip
  • Full Payment: Complete payment can be made during booking

6. Discounts and Offers

6.1 Regular Discounts

  • First-time customer discount: Up to 15% off
  • Frequent traveler discounts: Loyalty rewards program
  • Group booking discounts: For 3+ vehicles
  • Round-trip discounts: 10-15% off return journey

6.2 Promotional Offers

Seasonal promotions, festival offers, and special discount codes may be available. Terms and conditions apply to all promotional offers.

7. Pricing Transparency

7.1 Upfront Pricing

WaveTravel provides detailed fare breakdown before booking confirmation, including:

  • Base fare calculation
  • Additional charges (if any)
  • Tax breakdown
  • Total payable amount

7.2 No Hidden Charges

Guarantee: The final fare will not exceed the quoted price, except for additional services requested during the trip or unforeseen circumstances like extended waiting time.

8. Fare Dispute Resolution

In case of fare disputes:

  • Contact customer support within 24 hours of trip completion
  • Provide booking details and specific concern
  • We will review and resolve within 48-72 hours
  • Refunds (if applicable) processed within 5-7 business days

9. Price Updates

WaveTravel reserves the right to update pricing based on:

  • Fuel price fluctuations
  • Government policy changes
  • Market conditions
  • Operational cost changes

Price changes will be communicated through our website and app. Existing bookings will not be affected by price updates.
